PlantGrow

Water plants live with Bitcoin-Lightning donations


How it works:


1. You pay the lightning invoice

2. The lightning-switch detects your payment

3. The switch powers the water pump after 5s delay

4. You watered a plant somewhere else on planet earth
instant & permissionless with #Bitcoin
